Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

StepPet ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Info: Email address (for account management)
  • Identifiers: User ID (for account management and app functionality)
  • Purchases: Purchase history of gem packages or subscriptions
  • User Content: Pet data such as name, type, level, accessories, and progress
  • Friends List: (if applicable) usernames or IDs of friends in the app
  • Fitness Data: Step count data from your device
  • Diagnostics: Crash and performance data (for debugging and improvements)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the StepPet app
  • Personalize your experience
  • Allow pet evolution and game functionality
  • Enable friend competitions
  • Manage purchases and account services
  • Improve performance and fix bugs

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share minimal data with third-party services only to provide core app functionality (e.g., authentication, in-app purchases, analytics).

5. Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@steppet.app.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@steppet.app
